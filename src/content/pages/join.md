Public gatherings will be listed on the **Events** page when registration opens.
Each listed event links to its Luma registration page, where timing and attendance
details are kept current.

## Propose a session

If you want to host or suggest an experiment, prepare a short proposal:

- What question should the gathering examine?
- What format would help: conversation, workshop, Q&A, practice session, or coworking?
- What boundaries would make participation clear and safe?
- What, if anything, should be captured in a public log afterward?

Contact details for proposals will be added once the public intake channel is
chosen. Private group invites and meeting links are intentionally not posted here.
