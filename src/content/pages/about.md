Epistemic Experiments is a community for people who are thinking carefully about how to live, build, and make sense of what is happening, and who want to do that in good company.

## The spirit of the room

We value intellectual sincerity, curiosity, and care for the people in the
conversation. The goal is not to perform certainty. It is to try useful formats,
notice what actually happens, and improve the next attempt.

## Public memory, with boundaries

This site records public events and selected experiment logs. It does not expose
private group spaces, participant contact details, raw meeting links, or sensitive
conversation transcripts. Some learning is shareable; some trust depends on not
publishing.

## Propose a session

If you want to host or suggest an experiment, prepare a short proposal:

- What question should the gathering examine?
- What format would help: conversation, workshop, Q&A, practice session, or coworking?
- What boundaries would make participation clear and safe?
- What, if anything, should be captured in a public log afterward?

To propose a session, join the community and DM an admin.
